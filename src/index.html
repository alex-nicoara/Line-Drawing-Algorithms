Welcome! In this application, you can draw lines (using the Bresenham algorithm) and triangles using the scanline method. Click the headers to enter the coordinates and fill color of the object you desire to create. You can also randomly generate either lines or triangles by clicking the randomize button. To pause the animation, click the pause button.
